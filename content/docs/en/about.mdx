**NurimanPay** is a digital platform developed to simplify the management, transparency, and distribution of donations and alms (*infaq*) in a structured way. We exist to eliminate donor doubt by providing a well-documented reporting system accessible in real-time.

## **Our Mission**
We focus on solving the core issues in the donation world—transparency and trust—through:
* **Donation Digitalization:** Transforming manual methods into an integrated digital system using modern technology.
* **Public Accountability:** Ensuring every incoming fund is clearly recorded, from the target goal to physical proof of distribution.
* **Ease of Access:** Providing an optimal user experience through a simple interface friendly to all users.

## **Key Features for Donors**
You can create a real impact through:
1.  **Program Exploration:** Choose active programs such as Renovation Waqf, Blessed Food Sharing, or Student Scholarships.
2.  **Integrated Donation System:** Instant payments via QRIS, Bank Transfer, or E-Wallets with automatic validation.
3.  **Transparent Reports:** View fund distribution details including dates, amounts, and photographic documentation as a form of accountability.
