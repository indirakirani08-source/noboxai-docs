Transparency is our top priority. This page displays the tangible proof of the kindness you have shared, where collected funds have been fully distributed to the beneficiaries.

## **Completed Programs List**

Once a program reaches its donation target (100%), it is moved to the **Completed & Distributed Programs** category. Here, you can view the final summary of each initiative.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Completed%20Program.png"
  alt="NurimanPay Completed Programs List"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

In this section, each program card displays:
- **"Selesai" (Completed) Label:** Indicating that the fundraising period has ended.
- **Total Distributed:** The final amount of funds given to the beneficiaries.
- **100% Indicator:** Showing that the trust has been fully fulfilled according to the target.
- **Distribution Details Button:** Click this button to view the complete accountability report.
