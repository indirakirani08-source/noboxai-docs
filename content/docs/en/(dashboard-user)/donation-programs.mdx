The Donation Programs page is where you can explore various social initiatives that need your support. We provide transparent information so you can donate with confidence.

## **Available Programs List**

In this section, you will see cards of currently active programs. Each card provides a brief summary of the objectives and current funding status.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Program%20Donasi.png"

  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

Elements you can find in the program list:
- **Program Title:** The name of the initiative (e.g., Student Scholarships, Mosque Renovation).
- **Short Description:** A concise explanation of the program's background.
- **Progress Indicator:** Displays the percentage of funds collected compared to the target.
- **Donation Button:** Click "Donasi Sekarang" to view more details.

---

## **Donation Program Details**

Once you select a program, you will be directed to the detail page. This page is designed to provide an in-depth understanding before you make a contribution.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Detail%20Program.png"
  alt="NurimanPay Donation Program Details"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

### **Available Information:**

1. **Primary Information:** Displays the main photo, full title, and program category.
2. **Funding Statistics:**
   - **Collected:** The total amount of money received so far.
   - **Target:** The total funds required to complete the program.
   - **Donors:** The number of people who have already participated.
3. **Program Description:** A comprehensive explanation of the current conditions, fund allocation plans, and the expected benefits.
4. **Recent Donors:** A list of the most recent donors as a form of transparency and appreciation for their contributions.
5. **Action Button:** The "Donasi Sekarang" button to open the transaction form.

---

<Callout type="info">
  All funding data on the detail page is updated automatically and in real-time as soon as your payment is confirmed by the system.
</Callout>
