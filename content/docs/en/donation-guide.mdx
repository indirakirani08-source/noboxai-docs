## **How to Donate**

### **1. Access NurimanPay Website**
Open the browser on your mobile phone or computer, then visit the **[NurimanPay](https://nuriman-fe.vercel.app/)** website. You can complete your transaction directly without downloading any additional apps.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Hero%20Section.png"
  alt="NurimanPay Home Page"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

### **2. Select a Donation Program**
Find and choose the donation program you wish to support, then click the **Donate Now** button.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Program%20Donasi.png"
  alt="Select Donation Program"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

### **3. View Program Details**
Before proceeding, you can review the complete information regarding:
- Program title
- Program description/activity details
- Funding target
- Total funds collected
- List of other donors

Once you are sure, click the **Donate Now** button again.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Detail%20Program.png"
  alt="Program Details"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

### **4. Fill Out the Donation Form**
Complete the required information:
- **Amount:** Choose a preset quick amount or enter a custom amount as you wish.
- **Name:** Enter your full name.
- **WhatsApp Number:** For receipt or notification purposes.
- **Message/Prayer (Optional):** Words of support or prayers for the program.

After finishing, click **Continue Donation** to proceed to the payment stage.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Modal%20Transaksi.png"
  alt="Donor Data Form"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

### **5. Choose Payment Method**
Please select the payment method that is most convenient for you:
- **Bank Transfer (VA):** via a Virtual Account number.
- **Credit/Debit Card.**
- **QRIS:** Scan using your banking app or e-wallet (GoPay, OVO, ShopeePay, etc.).
- **E-Wallet.**

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Metode.png"
  alt="Select Payment Method"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

### **6. Complete the Payment**
For example, if you choose **BRI Bank Transfer**, simply copy the **Virtual Account** number that appears.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Pay.png"
  alt="VA Payment"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "

#### **Payment Simulation (Midtrans Sandbox)**
*Specifically for the testing phase, follow these steps:*
1. Open the Midtrans Sandbox simulator page.
2. Select the payment method you previously chose.
3. Paste the **Virtual Account** number.
4. Click the **Inquire** button.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Proses%20Pembayaran.png"
  alt="Payment Process"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

#### **Confirm Payment**
1. Double-check if the name and donation amount are correct.
2. Click the **Pay** button to complete the transaction.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Sandbox.png"
  alt="Payment Confirmation"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

#### **Check Payment Status**
1. Return to the NurimanPay website.
2. Click the **Check Status** button to update your payment information.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Cek%20Status.png"
  alt="Check Status"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

---

#### **Transaction Result**
1. You will see information whether the payment was Successful or Failed.
2. If successful, click **OK**.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Midtrans%20Hasil.png"
  alt="Payment Status"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

After clicking OK, you will be redirected back to the main website. A notification alert will appear; please click **Close**.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/en/Notifikasi%20Transaksi.png"
  alt="Transaction Notification"

  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4 my-8 mx-auto "
/>

<Callout type="info" title="Important Information">
  Currently, the payment system is still in the **Sandbox (Testing)** phase and does not use real currency (Production).
</Callout>

<Tips>
  **Donation Tips**
  - Use quick amount options for a faster process.
  - Ensure your internet connection is stable during payment.
  - Complete the payment promptly before the time limit expires.
</Tips>

<Kendala>
  **Help Center**
  - **Status hasn't changed?** Wait 1–3 minutes, then refresh the page.
  - **QR Code hard to scan?** Try increasing your phone screen's brightness.
  - **Payment failed?** Try repeating the process with a different payment method.
</Kendala>
