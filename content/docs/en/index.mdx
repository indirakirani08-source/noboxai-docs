Welcome to the official **NurimanPay** documentation. This platform was developed as a **Financial Technology (Fintech) Revolution** for the younger generation to simplify the management, transparency, and distribution of donations and alms (*infaq*) in a structured and trusted manner.

Through this documentation, you will understand how we address the challenges of transparency and accountability in the donation process through a clear and real-time reporting system.

---

## **About NurimanPay**

NurimanPay is a digital platform born from the need for a more professional and integrated donation management system. This project serves as a primary solution to overcome fundamental issues in the donation world: **trust** and **transparency**.

Developed by team **CC26-PS070** as part of the *Coding Camp 2026 powered by DBS Foundation*, this platform ensures that every contribution is well-documented and easily accessible to the public.

---

## **Our Key Features**

At NurimanPay, donors can clearly review program details before contributing. Our main features include:

* **Program List:** Displays various social initiatives and donation programs requiring support.
* **Fund Distribution Reports:** Detailed transparency reports including dates, purposes, amounts, descriptions, and photographic proof of distribution.
* **Real-time Monitoring:** Donors can monitor the progress and accountability of fund management directly through the system.

---

## **Technology & Reliability**

The platform is built with industry standards to ensure high performance:

* **Backend:** Built using the **Express Framework** with **RESTful API** implementation.
* **Frontend:** Powered by **Vite** with a responsive layout for all device sizes.
* **Security:** Rigorous input validation to guarantee user data security.
* **Deployment:** Hosted on **Vercel** for stable public access.

---

## **Core Values**

1.  **High Transparency:** Detailed reporting to maintain and enhance donor trust.
2.  **Seamless User Experience:** Simple and user-friendly interface designed for everyone.
3.  **Real Social Impact:** Addressing social participation issues by ensuring accountable fund management.
4.  **System Stability:** Focus on core functions to ensure a reliable and glitch-free experience.
